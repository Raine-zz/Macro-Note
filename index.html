Overview Page

What is Macroeconomics?

The most common definition is: Macroeconomics is the study of the behaviour of a national or regional economy as a whole.

But as Blanchard said, "The best way to answer is not to give you a formal definition, but rather to take you on an economic tour of the world."

For me, "A model can describe world" is an exciting thing. Hence, I created this webpage to record the macroeconomics tools that I have learned. Hoping I can continue to explore in the field of macroeconomics.
